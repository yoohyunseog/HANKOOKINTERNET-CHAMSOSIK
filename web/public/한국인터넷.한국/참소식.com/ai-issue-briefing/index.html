1) 캐릭터 테이블

  • character_id (PK)
  • nickname
  • level
  • status_type
  • N_value
  • visit_count

2) 사건 테이블

  • event_id (PK)
  • character_id (FK)
  • event_summary
  • keywords
  • emotion
  • created_at

3) 장면 테이블

  • scene_id (PK)
  • event_id (FK)
  • scene_summary
  • scene_status
  • next_scene_hint
  • N_value

4) 관계 테이블

  • relation_id (PK)
  • target_type
  • relation_type
  • relation_value
  • memo
  • created_at
